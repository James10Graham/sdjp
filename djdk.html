DJ DOUBLE K

Double K is the gentle giant of the crew he is a DJ/Producer and loves playing Funky House. The man is simply a genius it was Double K that started the Shropshire DJ’s Podcast due to his sheer love for all things music. Double K is the spine of the podcast and holds his idea close to heart. Double K mixes in the city of Nottingham and has been known to absolutely rock the venue using seamless mixes and a heavy artillery of non-stop bangers. Double K’s love for music also floods into his creative side and has a keen ear to produce.

Double K enjoys making music and enjoys the reaction of the crew when he drops his latest track. Double K is very keen to work with up and coming artists in all genres.
