E-VOLVE

Although E-Volve plays no music or mixes and blends E-Volve has a very keen ear and is able to express his views eloquently. E-Volve loves old skool classics and some of the latest new artists that have talent. E-Volve when he was much younger had a few shows on a pirate radio station he also used to manage a band known as the Soul Preachers who were destined for big things.

Music is a big feature in his life and was approached by Double K to get involved in the podcast. Due to other commitments this was not on his radar. After some persuasion became involved with the show. E-Volve is a music visionary and help to propel the show with multiple ideas.
