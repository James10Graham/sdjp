COMIC MARVEL

ComicMarvel is the quick witted joker of the crew. Who has a contagious high energy who possesses a musical knowledge that is varied. ComicMarvel is a DJ and started mixing and scratching in the early 90’s and since then has been collecting vinyl music ever since and has an extensive collection. Marvel is a Retro Funk Junkie that likes to mix the old with the new this will often come out in the music he plays and the sets that he mixes. If you were to see a track listing before it is played you would possibly consider not staying around, but with clever mixing and scratching he will capture a audience and have you rocking till tha early morn’.
